I help people and organizations rewire patterns—combining clinical psychotherapy with mindfulness, neuroplasticity practices, and practical success psychology. Comnmon therapy is built on Cognitivbe Behavioral Therapy developed in the 1950s and 1960s by Beck and Ellis. The principle is that behavior comes from emotion and emotion comes from thought. But what does thought come from? According to the latest research, our thoughts are developed by our mindset. By transforming our mindset, our core beliefs about ourselves and success - we can create new and better versions of our lives..
