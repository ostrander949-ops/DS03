Who we are

Science. Compassion. Results.

Discovery Science is a remote brain training company focused on personalized protocols, measurable progress, and practical support. We combine structured intake, home-based EEG technology, app-guided training, and coaching oversight into a single client experience built for real-world outcomes.

Our aim is simple: help clients regulate attention, arousal, stress response, and recovery patterns more effectively without requiring repeated in-office visits. Every program is designed around the client’s needs, goals, symptom profile, and performance data.

How it works

Your personalized brain training journey

Every new client begins with intake and assessment, followed by protocol design, at-home setup, data review, and ongoing coaching support.

1

Intake & Assessment

Complete a structured intake, symptom review, history summary, and baseline evaluation to clarify your needs and priorities.

2

Personalized Plan

Your program design is configured around the presentation, goals, and training strategy that best fits your case.

3

Train From Home

Use your personal EEG device and custom app from the comfort of home with no routine office visits required.

4

Monitor Progress

Review trend data, symptom check-ins, and adherence to determine what is working and what should be refined.

5

Achieve Results

Build more stable, trainable patterns over time with the goal of improved daily functioning and quality of life.
