AI and the Erosion of Human Trust in Commercial Dialogue

Discovery Science Institute — Research Note

Abstract

Artificial intelligence is rapidly transforming the structure of professional communication. Automated outreach, synthetic messaging, and algorithmic qualification systems now mediate many early interactions between organizations and potential clients. While these tools improve efficiency, they may also undermine the relational signals that allow trust to form between human actors.

Automation and Authenticity

Human communication contains subtle cues indicating attention, effort, and presence. When messages are automated, these cues can disappear, producing a sense of impersonality or manipulation even when the message content itself is reasonable.

Over time this may create systemic distrust toward institutional communication.

The Paradox of AI Communication

AI tools promise personalization at scale, yet true personalization requires genuine awareness of another person’s context. Algorithmic systems approximate this awareness using data signals, but they cannot replicate the lived experience of human relational understanding.

Future Research

Future work at Discovery Science will explore how organizations can integrate AI tools while preserving authentic human dialogue and relational trust.
