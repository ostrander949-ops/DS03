Contact the Institute

Discovery Science welcomes thoughtful conversations with researchers, writers, founders, organizations, and collaborators interested in human communication, neurodiversity, ethical persuasion, contemplative inquiry, and trust in an AI-mediated world.

Reasons to reach out

Common reasons people contact the institute include:

  • Research collaboration or intellectual partnership
  • Interviews or writing collaborations
  • Discussion of neurodivergence and communication research
  • Exploring the Humanistic Selling methodology
  • Selective advisory or coaching inquiries
  • Speaking or educational invitations
