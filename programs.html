Programs

Personalized programs for attention, stress regulation, mood, and recovery.

Discovery Science offers remote brain training pathways designed around the client’s goals, symptoms, baseline patterns, and progress over time. Each program is delivered from home using a personal EEG device and a customized app workflow.
