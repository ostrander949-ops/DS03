One platform. Two apps.

A clinician view for care teams and a consumer app for clients practicing at home.

Clinician Dashboard

  • Assign tracks: calm, focus, sleep descent, regulation, meditation
  • Monitor adherence: sessions completed, minutes trained, missed days
  • Review progress: client trends, session scores, response patterns
  • Support care: exportable reports for treatment reviews and client feedback

Client App

  • Guided home sessions: prescribed by the clinic
  • Closed-loop feedback: audio cues adapt to state changes
  • Simple scores: no confusing raw EEG charts
  • Progress visibility: clients see streaks, milestones, and improvements
